What is this about???

It's a program that makes wool from numbers!

Instructions

Click to set a number spinning off in a random direction. The number is weakly attracted to the pointer. If you click a lot you can develop a wonderful bouquet of numbers that leave wooly trails. The rotation speed is roughly set at the number displayed, in degrees per second.

Press R to switch the rotation units between radians per second and degrees per second.

Press D to detonate a number into its colour components!

Press I to toggle between wool and colourful light trails!

Press C to switch between HSB and RGB modes, for some reason!
